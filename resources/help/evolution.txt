The evolution simulation based on a habitat with 80 x 80 cells.
In these cells are live or die creatures like protozoa.
Protozoa is a one cell creature and can live in cell colonies.

Colonies are:

    Blinker
    Bipole
    Clock
    Octogone
    Pentomino
    Porpoise
    Pulsator
    Simple
    Skid
    Toad
    Tripole

In a biotope can live many colonies.
